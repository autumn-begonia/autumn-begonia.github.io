秋海棠

个人简介: 秋海棠，出生于1965年10月15日，籍贯广东省江门市新会区，本人是一位小学高级教师(已退休)。平时热爱诗词及文章创作，多年来创作了海量原创诗词，还有散文、杂文、链接评论等······希望通过这些作品，传递对生活的热爱和对大自然的赞美，以及抒发对现实中杰出人物的崇敬之情，传播正能量。同时希望能得到大家的共鸣与支持，从而激发出无穷的创作动力来创造精品。

Autumn Begonia

Personal Introduction: Autumn Begonia, born on October 15, 1965, from Xinhui District, Jiangmen City, Guangdong Province. I am a retired senior elementary school teacher. I love creating poetry and articles. Over the years, I have created a vast amount of original poetry, essays, critiques, and more... Through these works, I hope to convey my love for life, admiration for nature, and respect for outstanding individuals in reality, spreading positive energy. I also hope to resonate with and gain support from everyone, thus inspiring endless creative motivation to produce excellent works.
